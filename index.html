Research interests

My research focuses on evaluating and applying advanced statistical methods to behavioral data. Behavioral data displays certain qualities (e.g., categorical variables, non-normally distributed variables, frequent clustering of individuals within families, schools, or neighborhoods, missing values) that require specialized statistical methods and approaches.
